Here in this section you can ask for your question or suggestion or any kind of inquiry and our volunteer team would try to contact with you and clearify your questions.

Question About - Receive Medicine

For receiving medicine, you don't need to open account. You have to bring the prescribed medicine to the NGO's office from any registered doctor or any doctor who has account in our web-portal.

Question About - Donate Medicine

For donating medicine, you need to create an account and fill up a form about the medicine description. The medicine expiry date should be more than 2 months and you need to give us the pick up address.

Question About - Doctor's Subscription

Only registered doctors can become members by creating account in our system and prescribed free medicines for their poop patients.
